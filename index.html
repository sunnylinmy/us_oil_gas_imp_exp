About

Data used in this data visualization is from the International Trade API on US Census Bureau's website. Oil and gas extraction definition here follows 2012 NAICS: 2111.This visualization was created by Mengyang Lin at the Duke University Energy Initiative using d3.js and d3-legend.js.
